Why Luxembourg
The natural gateway to European institutional capital.
The world's second-largest fund domicile with €5.7 trillion in assets under management across 3,900+ fund structures.
01

Fund Industry Scale

€5.7 trillion AUM across 3,900+ structures. Largest fund domicile in Europe, second globally.

02

EIB & EIF Headquarters

The EU's primary long-term financing institutions are based here — the natural origination point for EU-backed capital.

03

Private Equity Density

300+ PE and VC managers with significant exposure to infrastructure, buyout, and growth equity across CEE.

04

Family Office Community

Growing network of single- and multi-family offices with increasing allocation to EU convergence markets.

05

UCITS & AIF Ecosystem

Global centre for cross-border fund distribution — UCITS across 70+ jurisdictions and full alternative spectrum.

06

Sovereign & Institutional Access

Direct proximity to EIB, EIF, EFSI, and multilateral capital pools targeting EU convergence regions.
